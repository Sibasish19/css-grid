By default, the implicitly defined cells flow to another row. You can change this behavior by setting grid-auto-flow to column.

In geneal the grid items flow per cell until it encounters a cell big enough for the avaiable area then it flows to the next row by default. With grid-auto-flow set to desnse, it will fill the blank space with the next items that can fit.
